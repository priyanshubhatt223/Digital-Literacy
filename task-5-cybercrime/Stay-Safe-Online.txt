Fraud Prevention Tips :-

1. Use strong, unique passwords for each account (mix of letters, numbers, symbols) and enable 2-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
2. Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank or official service.
3. Verify links before clicking—avoid suspicious emails, messages, or unknown attachments.
4. Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
5. Use secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi or use a VPN.
6. Limit personal information sharing on social media (phone number, address, college ID, etc.).
7. Log out from shared/public computers and avoid saving passwords on them.
8. Download apps only from trusted sources like official app stores and check permissions carefully.

UPI & Financial Safety Tips :-

1. Double-check UPI IDs and QR codes before making payments—fraudsters often use similar-looking IDs.
2. Remember: receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN—never approve unknown “collect” requests.
3. Enable transaction alerts (SMS/email) to monitor any unauthorized activity.
4. Avoid screen sharing during banking transactions—scammers may trick you into revealing sensitive info.

In Case of Cyber Fraud :-

1. Call Helpline: 1930 immediately (for quick action on financial fraud)
2. Report online at: National Cyber Crime Portal → https://cybercrime.gov.in
