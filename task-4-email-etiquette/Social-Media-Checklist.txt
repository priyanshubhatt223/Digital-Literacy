DO's :-

1. Your post and comments should be respectful and should not hurt someone's feelings.
2. Make sure your privacy settings are safe.
3. Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
4. Use social media for learning, networking, and positive engagement.
5. be respectful even while disagreeing with someone.

DON'Ts :-

1. Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly.
2. Don’t engage in cyberbullying or offensive behavior.
3. Don’t believe or spread fake news without checking sources.
4. Don’t overshare content that could harm your reputation.
5. Don’t spend excessive time on social media that affects studies or health.
